Privacy Policy

App Name: Speedy – Precision GPS Speedometer

Developer: Muhammed Shibil

Last updated: 21 February 2026

1. Introduction

Speedy (“the App”) is a GPS-based speedometer application designed to measure speed, distance, and trip duration. This Privacy Policy explains how user and device data is handled.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects the following data:

  • Location data (GPS) to calculate speed, distance, and trip duration
  • App usage data required for core functionality

The App does not collect personal identifiers such as name, phone number, email address, or contacts.

3. Location Data Usage

Location access is required for the App to function correctly. Location data is used only while the App is active and a trip is in progress.

The App does not track location in the background.

4. Data Storage

All trip data (such as speed history, distance, and duration) is stored locally on the user’s device.

Users can delete all stored trip data at any time from within the App settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers, network information, and usage data to serve ads.

Data collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google’s own privacy policy.

6. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell or share user data. Any data collected by third-party services is handled according to their respective privacy policies.

7. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. User Control

Users can start or stop trip tracking at any time and can clear all stored data from the App.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
